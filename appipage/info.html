Sāhityadarpaṇa of Viśvanātha, (1936 edition)

app1 provides access to pages based on pariccheda (1-10) and kārikā.
https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/sahityadarpana_mw/app1/?N,N
Example: https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/sahityadarpana_mw/?app1/1,3

appipage provides access based on internal page number (2-632).
It is used by app1 to go to next or previous page.
https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/sahityadarpana_mw/appipage/?N
Example: https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/sahityadarpana_mw/appipage/?200
